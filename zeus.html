Event List

There's a bunch of events that can be triggered

There's a bunch of event's that can be triggered into the game,
To trigger one of the events you have to make sure you have enough points.
You can check your points by typing '!points' in chat.

If you have enough points you can type '!redeem eventName <amount>' in chat.
Here's a list of different redeems you can trigger using ZEUS_B0T.
